🔍 The Problem

⏱️

Time Wasted

Urban commuters lose an average of 54 hours per year stuck in traffic. That's over 2 full days of life wasted annually.

🌍

CO₂ Emissions

Traffic congestion contributes to 29% of greenhouse gas emissions in urban areas, accelerating climate change.

💸

Economic Cost

Congestion costs the global economy over $1 trillion annually in lost productivity and fuel waste.

😤

Quality of Life

Noise pollution, stress, and reduced accessibility hurt the mental and physical health of millions of city dwellers.

📋 Empathy Research Summary

We conducted 15 interviews with daily commuters in Paris, Lyon, and Reims. Key insights:

  • "I'd carpool but I never know who's going my way at the same time."
  • "Traffic lights seem random — green when no one's there, red when the road is packed."
  • "I'd use a scooter for short trips but I don't know where to find one reliably."
  • "There's no single app that shows me the best option for my commute right now."

Ideation phase: we brainstormed 12 ideas and converged on 3 complementary pillars.

Smart Traffic Lights

AI-powered traffic signals that adapt in real-time to traffic flow using sensors and camera data. Reduces average wait time by up to 40%.

  • ✅ Real-time density detection
  • ✅ Emergency vehicle priority
  • ✅ Pedestrian-aware timing
  • ✅ Connected corridor sync
