⚙️ How It Works

Our AI-powered pipeline in 4 steps

1

📷 Data Collection

Cameras, IoT sensors, and GPS data feed real-time information into our system from thousands of points across the city.

2

🧠 AI Analysis

Deep learning models predict traffic patterns 30 minutes ahead, analyzing flow density, speed, and incidents.

3

🚦 Adaptive Signals

Traffic lights dynamically adjust timing based on real-time demand. Green waves are created for optimal flow.

4

📊 Continuous Learning

The system learns from every cycle, improving predictions and reducing congestion further each day.
