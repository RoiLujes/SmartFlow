How SmartFlow Works

From sensor data to optimized traffic in milliseconds

1

📷 Sense

Cameras and IoT sensors detect vehicle count, speed, and queue length at every intersection in real time.

2

🧠 Analyze

AI processes data from all connected intersections simultaneously, predicting congestion before it happens.

3

⚡ Adapt

Signal timings adjust dynamically — extending green for heavy flow, creating green waves for emergency vehicles.

4

📈 Learn

The system learns from every decision, improving predictions for rush hours, events, and weather conditions.
