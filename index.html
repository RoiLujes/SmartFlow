How SmartFlow Works

Four layers of intelligence powering every intersection

1

📷 Detect

Computer vision cameras detect and classify every vehicle, pedestrian, and cyclist approaching the intersection in real time.

2

🧮 Predict

ML models analyze historical data, weather, events, and current flow to forecast traffic 5-15 minutes ahead.

3

⚡ Optimize

A reinforcement learning agent dynamically adjusts signal timing, phase sequencing, and multi-intersection coordination.

4

🔄 Learn

Every outcome feeds back into the system. SmartFlow gets smarter with every cycle, continuously improving performance.
